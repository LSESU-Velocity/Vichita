description: Use when preparing marketing operations rows, launch gates, or campaign task tracking.
---

Vichita may create marketing operations rows and launch gates after approval.

It should not generate generic social captions, posters, or image assets as a core workflow.

Promotion remains blocked until the event form is submitted and SU approval is clear.

Apply extra gates only where they are relevant:

- Speaker gate: required only when external speakers are involved.
- Sponsor gate: required only when sponsor branding, sponsor deliverables, or sponsor approval is involved.
- Ticketing gate: required only when ticketing, paid entry, capacity control, or access registration is involved.
- Room gate: required when an LSE room booking or room confirmation is needed.
