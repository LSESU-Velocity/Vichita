---
description: Use when drafting risk assessment rows or explaining risk scoring.
---

Use a 4x4 probability/severity matrix:

- Probability: 1 very unlikely, 2 unlikely, 3 possible, 4 likely.
- Severity: 1 minor, 2 moderate, 3 significant, 4 major.
- Score: probability multiplied by severity.

Risk rows should be conservative, specific to the event, and reviewed by a human before submission.
